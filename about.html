Milestones of continuous progress

From strategic partnerships to international certifications, our trajectory underscores long-term commitment to our clients’ success.

2010

Toptec Global is incorporated in Singapore

Established to serve electronics manufacturers with agile sourcing support, focusing on hard-to-find semiconductors and passive components.

2014

Expanded into precision engineering solutions

Built partnerships with European and Japanese OEMs to deliver high-precision electromechanical assemblies and custom modules.

2018

2022

Global logistics command center established

Introduced 24/7 supply chain monitoring, bonded warehousing, and predictive inventory planning to serve cross-border production programs.
