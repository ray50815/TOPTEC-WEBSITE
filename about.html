Corporate overview

Dual-core excellence in electronics and trading.

From Singapore, Toptec Global delivers precision switches for devices and governs petroleum and quartz sand supply chains with HSSE and compliance at the core.

Connect with our leadership

Two divisions, one governance model

Electronics builds tactile, slide, and micro switches with automotive-grade reliability; Trading governs petroleum and industrial minerals with HSSE, sanctions, and transparency.

Electronics Division. Design-in support for control switches with defined actuation force, IP67 options, PPAP, and traceability for automotive and IoT programs.

Trading Division. Petroleum products and high-purity quartz sand with lab certification, sanctions screening, and 24/7 logistics control for energy and infrastructure.

Governance office. Embedded HSSE, AML/KYC, and regulator-ready reporting across both divisions to protect stakeholders.

Milestones in dual-core growth
