National utility fuel security

A national utility required assured diesel under regulator scrutiny. We activated pre-cleared suppliers, deployed floating storage, and synchronized customs and HSSE controls within 72 hours.

  • 12 voyages with regulator-witnessed QC and digital reporting
  • Zero audit findings with full cost recovery and no demurrage
  • HSSE compliance across chartered assets and bonded storage

Automotive tactile switch customization

Designed a high-force tactile switch for a premium EV dashboard, tuning actuation force, travel, and contact resistance while meeting IP67 and PPAP requirements.

  • Force-displacement curves tuned to OEM haptic spec
  • PPAP, life cycle to 1M actuations, and IP67 validation
  • SMT assembly guidance and lot-level traceability

Solar glass silica supply chain

A sovereign-backed solar glass producer required ultra-low iron silica with full transparency. We validated sources, controlled moisture onsite, and synchronized barge deliveries to the furnace plan.

  • 99.7% SiO2 and Fe2O3 below 110 ppm from ISO/IEC 17025 labs
  • Daily compliance reporting to the government project office
  • Zero furnace downtime with regulator-ready QA documentation

Smart home IoT controller reliability

Achieved a 1M-cycle lifespan for a smart thermostat rotary dial by optimizing contact resistance, bounce, and seal design for IP67 in high-humidity environments.

  • Reliability rigs validated 1M+ actuations with stable contact resistance
  • Ingress protection and environmental cycling for high-humidity installs
  • Assembly and PCB footprint guidance to reduce field failures
