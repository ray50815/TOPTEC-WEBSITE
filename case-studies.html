Accelerated automotive electronics supply by 30%

Industry: Automotive electronics Region: Southeast Asia

Challenge: “Our Tier-1 OEM faced chronic MCU shortages delaying product launches.”

Solution: Implemented alternate component validation, established buffer inventory, and coordinated vendor-managed stock programs.

Result: Lead times reduced from 8 weeks to 5 weeks, with annual savings of USD 1.2M.

Client: Helix Automotive Systems

Stabilized semiconductor fab operations

Industry: Semiconductor manufacturing Region: East Asia

Challenge: “Unplanned downtime from specialty chemical and wafer shortages.”

Solution: Diversified supplier base, implemented predictive inventory analytics, and secured bonded warehousing near the fab.

Result: Achieved 99.2% uptime and improved supplier OTIF by 18%.

Client: Orion Semicon

Optimized industrial automation MRO strategy

Industry: Industrial automation Region: Europe

Challenge: “Sporadic downtime from lack of critical drive assemblies.”

Solution: Consolidated suppliers, introduced predictive maintenance spares, and provided on-site technical training.

Result: Reduced unplanned outages by 45% and increased maintenance productivity.

Client: Vantage Robotics Automation
