Emergency diesel compliance program for a national utility

A Southeast Asian power authority required emergency diesel deliveries under strict regulator oversight. We activated pre-cleared suppliers, deployed floating storage, and coordinated customs clearance within 72 hours.

  • 12 emergency voyages with regulator-witnessed QC & reporting
  • Emergency audit closed with zero findings and full cost recovery
  • 0 demurrage or HSSE incidents across chartered assets

Compliance-ready silica for a sovereign-backed glass expansion

A sovereign-backed float-glass producer required ultra-low iron silica with full transparency for parliamentary review. We validated sources, installed on-site moisture controls, and synchronised barge deliveries with the furnace commissioning plan.

  • 99.7% SiO2 with Fe2O3 below 110 ppm validated by ISO/IEC 17025 labs
  • Dedicated compliance officer delivering daily updates to the government project office
  • Zero furnace downtime across commissioning run with full compliance reporting

Fuel security & compliance for public transit

A metropolitan transit agency needed to secure diesel supply while satisfying transport ministry oversight. We introduced hedging, bonded storage, and a shared compliance portal for regulators.

  • 45-day rolling stock maintained with documented reserve testing
  • Price volatility reduced by 28% through coordinated hedging and transparent reporting
  • Compliance portal adopted by finance ministry and transport authority
