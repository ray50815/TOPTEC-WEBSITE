Featured bottom housing programs

Each project shows how we bring bottom housing designs from idea to production.

Cut cosmetic rejects for personal mobility bottom housings

Industry: Personal mobility devices Region: Southeast Asia

Challenge: "Our e-scooter bottom housings were failing gloss and impact checks, delaying ramp."

Solution: Reworked tooling venting, introduced robotic sanding, and added inline gloss metering across each colorway.

Result: Cosmetic rejects dropped 42% and the ramp achieved full volume two weeks earlier.

Client: AN XIN

Launched multi-color smart-home hubs in eight weeks

Industry: Smart home & IoT Region: East Asia

Challenge: "Limited internal capacity to deliver new colorways with consistent finishes for a global launch."

Solution: Delivered rapid DFM, built multi-cavity steel, and established paint recipes with inline spectrophotometers.

Result: Produced 300k bottom housings with Delta E < 1 and shipped on the original launch schedule.

Client: COCOCLOUD

Enabled modular industrial console assemblies without delays

Industry: Industrial automation Region: Europe

Challenge: "Assembly teams struggled with misaligned mounting points and inconsistent hardware kits."

Solution: Converted to a hybrid CNC plus molding process, installed threaded inserts, and supplied kitted fasteners per line.

Result: Assembly time reduced 32% and in-plant rework dropped below 1%.

Client: WINTEAM

Delivered luxury gloss for a beauty device bottom housing

Industry: Beauty & wellness Region: North America

Challenge: "Need to achieve mirror gloss without swirl marks while meeting cosmetic QA across retail packaging."

Solution: Combined PVD base, UV clear coat, and cleanroom packaging with individual protective films.

Result: Achieved zero cosmetic returns after launch and supported a 120k unit rollout.
