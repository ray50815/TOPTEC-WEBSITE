Case studies

A sample of the transformations we have shipped with our partners.

AN XIN • Smart Factory Scheduling

Client: AN XIN

Industry: Precision metal machining

Toptec services: Factory data integration, scheduling AI, operator cockpit

Challenge

An Xin’s planners juggled purchase, CNC, and finishing schedules in disconnected spreadsheets, creating last-minute overtime and scrap risk.

Solution

Toptec wired ERP, MES, and sensor feeds into a digital twin that drives an optimisation engine; supervisors manage shifts through a bilingual cockpit with automatic alerts.

Results

  • Average lead time shortened by 22% within two quarters.
  • On-time shipments held steady at 97% after the first three months.
  • Line supervisors reclaimed two hours per shift previously spent on manual sequencing.

WINTEAM • Connected Kitchen Launch

Client: WINTEAM

Industry: Smart kitchen appliances

Toptec services: IoT orchestration, showroom analytics, service playbooks

Challenge

WINTEAM’s robotics line debuted across flagship stores, yet firmware updates, menu content, and maintenance tickets were all handled by separate teams without telemetry.

Solution

We delivered a central orchestration plane that synchronises device fleets, digital signage, and service diagnostics; field teams receive predictive alerts in a single app.

Results

  • Store configuration time dropped from four hours to forty-five minutes.
  • Demo-to-order conversions improved by 23% during the launch quarter.
  • Predictive maintenance avoided eighteen urgent service escalations in the first season.

RCEDAO • Regulated Treasury Automation
