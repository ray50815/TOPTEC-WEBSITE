Crude feedstock compliance program

Pain point: Emission caps and product specifications threatened by volatile crude assays.

Solution: Dual-grade crude slate, regulator-witnessed ISO/IEC 17025 labs, and emissions reporting dashboards.

Outcome: Maintained 98% utilisation with zero sulfur non-compliance notices and renewed supply licence.

Silica assurance for glass & solar

Pain point: Parliamentary oversight delaying approvals because of inconsistent silica purity.

Solution: High-purity silica below 110 ppm Fe2O3, moisture-controlled storage, and a shared QA portal for stakeholders.

Outcome: Expansion approved in record time and production uplift delivered without unplanned stoppages.

Utility fuel security & compliance

Pain point: Seasonal demand and storage constraints increasing scrutiny from energy regulators.

Solution: Hedged fuel oil and diesel supply with floating storage, emergency drawdown playbooks, and regulator briefing kits.

Outcome: Maintained 45 days forward cover with transparent reporting accepted by national energy regulators.

Public infrastructure aggregate governance

Pain point: Procurement auditors delaying approvals for aggregates on strategic infrastructure builds.

Solution: Tender-compliant procurement, ESG reporting, and milestone-based deliveries with photographic evidence for auditors.

Outcome: Approved within audit window and delivered 1.8M tonnes without variance penalties.
