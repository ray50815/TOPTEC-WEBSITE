Application playbooks

Every program addresses unique cosmetic, structural, and logistics needs so shipments arrive build-ready.

Smart home & IoT

Pain point: Frequent color refreshes and exacting cosmetic tolerances across high volumes.

Solution: Multi-cavity tooling with quick-change inserts, in-line color labs, and 100% visual inspection.

Result: Maintained Delta E <1 consistency while shipping 300k kits per month.

Personal mobility devices

Pain point: Structural strength and weather durability for high-load, outdoor use.

Solution: Aluminum and reinforced polymer assemblies validated through fatigue, salt spray, and impact testing.

Result: Extended field life by 25% and cleared regulatory drop tests on first submission.

Healthcare & wellness equipment

Pain point: Biocompatible surfaces and cleanroom-ready packaging for patient-facing devices.

Solution: Medical-grade coatings, UV disinfection, and ISO 13485 documentation packs.

Result: Accelerated regulatory review and achieved zero contamination deviations.

Industrial control & automation

Pain point: Low- to mid-volume custom enclosures with complex assembly requirements.

Solution: Flexible CNC plus molding mix, modular fixtures, and kitted fasteners for plug-and-play deployment.

Result: Cut assembly time by 32% and reduced in-plant rework to under 1%.

Premium consumer electronics

Pain point: Luxury-grade finishes and seamless fit for flagship product launches.

Solution: Glass bead blasting, PVD, and automated polishing backed by cosmetic SPC dashboards.

Result: Delivered scratch-free builds for a global launch across five regions.

Commercial appliances & retail tech

Pain point: Durable, service-friendly enclosures needed for high-traffic retail and hospitality environments.

Solution: Powder-coated metallic enclosures with quick-release panels, IP-rated sealing, and tamper-proof fasteners.

Result: Reduced service time by 40% and kept devices within cosmetic spec after 12 months in the field.

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the questions enclosure, mechanical, and NPI teams ask when partnering with us.

What materials and processes can you support for international programs? +

We work with aluminum, magnesium, stainless steel, ABS/PC blends, reinforced polymers, plus finishing from paint and hydrographic to anodizing and PVD.

How quickly can you provide production samples? +

Soft-tool samples ship in 10–15 business days, while multi-cavity production T1 samples typically ship within 25 days depending on complexity.

Can you manage assembly and packaging requirements? +

Yes. We install inserts, EMI gaskets, rubber feet, and deliver custom packaging, labels, and kitting aligned with your EMS line.

How do you ensure cosmetic consistency across large volumes? +

We maintain master panels, inline vision inspection, and SPC reporting, with corrective action loops tied to your quality team.
